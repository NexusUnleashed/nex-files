title: Room Players

Room Players lists players in the current room with ally/enemy formatting and hover details.

<!-- TODO: Add a screenshot of the Room Players tab. -->

## What it shows

- Players from `GMCP.Room.Players`.
- Sorting by enemy/ally/city priority, then alphabetically.
- Color formatting from the character database (CDB).

## Actions

- Click a player to run `settarget <name>`.
- Hover a player to open a popover with CDB fields.

## Notes

- Ally/enemy formatting is controlled by **Style** options.
- The popover hides `regex`, `user`, and `time` fields.
