Play Stats tracks instance, session, and monthly progress.

<!-- TODO: Add a screenshot of the Play Stats tab. -->

## What it shows

- Instance stats (since last reset).
- Session stats (since login).
- Monthly stats (from server month rollover).

## Data sources

- GMCP status updates for gold, XP, and achievements.
- `IRE.Time` events to detect month changes.

## Notes

- Monthly counters reset when the in-game month changes.
