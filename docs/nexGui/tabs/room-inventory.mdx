---
title: Room Inventory
---

Room Inventory lists all non-NPC items in the current room and exposes quick actions.

<!-- TODO: Add a screenshot of the Room Inventory tab. -->

## What it shows

- Items from `GMCP.Items.room` excluding NPC entries.
- Item ID and name.
- Color and label overrides from `nexGui.colors.room`.

## Actions

- Click an item to open the action menu.
- `Probe` is always available.
- `Get` appears when the item is takeable (`attrib` includes `t`).

## Notes

- Items are sorted with colored items and takeable items first.
