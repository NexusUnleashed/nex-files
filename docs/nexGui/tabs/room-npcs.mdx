title: Room NPCs
---

Room NPCs lists NPCs in the current room, grouped by type for faster target selection.

<!-- TODO: Add a screenshot of the Room NPCs tab. -->

## What it shows

- NPCs from `GMCP.Items.room` with `attrib` `m` or `mx`.
- Group headers for guards, entities, shipmates, and swashbucklers.

## Actions

- Click an NPC to run `settarget <npc id>`.
- Expand or collapse group sections to reduce noise.

## Notes

- City guards and special entities are filtered using known lists in the tab component.
