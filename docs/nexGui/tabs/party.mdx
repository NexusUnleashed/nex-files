Party provides lightweight party tracking and call management.

<!-- TODO: Add a screenshot of the Party tab. -->

## What it shows

- Party member list and current leader.
- Toggles for party call behavior.

## Actions

- Add a member by typing a name and pressing Enter.
- Click a member (not yourself) to remove them.
- Select a leader from the dropdown.

## Toggles

- Collect Gold
- Accept Targets
- Accept Affs
- Call Targets
- Call Affs
- Gag Chat

## Notes

- State is stored in `nexGui.party` and is shared across tabs.
